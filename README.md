# 🌆 Tapestry AI

Tapestry AI is a next-generation Smart City Civic Intelligence Platform designed to modernize civic issue reporting, infrastructure monitoring, and urban management through Artificial Intelligence, Geographic Information Systems (GIS), and Predictive Analytics.

---

## 🚀 Vision

Transform civic issue reporting from a reactive complaint system into a proactive infrastructure intelligence platform.

Citizens can report issues such as:

- Potholes
- Water Leakages
- Garbage Accumulation
- Broken Streetlights
- Drainage Issues
- Public Safety Hazards

while municipalities gain access to AI-powered insights, predictive maintenance recommendations, and real-time infrastructure analytics.
